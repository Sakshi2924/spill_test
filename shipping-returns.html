Shipping & Returns

Ship It

Shipping

We ship across India. Orders are dispatched within 2–3 business days. Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days depending on your location.

Tracking

Once your order ships you'll receive a tracking link via email so you can follow your tea on its journey.

Returns

Not vibing with your order? You can initiate a return within 7 days of delivery, provided the product is unopened and in its original packaging.

Damaged Goods

If your order arrives damaged, snap a photo and email us. We'll sort it out faster than you can brew a cup.

International

International shipping is coming soon. Want to be notified? Drop us a line.
