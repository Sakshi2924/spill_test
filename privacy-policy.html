Privacy Policy

Privacy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

When you interact with Spill we may collect your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information necessary to process your orders. We also collect basic technical information (IP address, browser, device) for security and analytics.

How We Use It

Your information helps us fulfil orders, improve our products, and keep you updated on new brews. We never sell your data to third parties.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and understand how you interact with our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some parts of the site may not function without them.

Security

We use industry-standard protections — TLS encryption in transit, hashed credentials, rate limiting, and access controls — to keep your data safe. No system is perfect, so we also keep our stack updated and monitored.

Third-Party Processors

We work with payment processors and shipping partners that handle your data under their own privacy terms. We select vendors with strong track records and share only the minimum information needed.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Reach us at hello@spillnow.in.
