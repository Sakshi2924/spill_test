The Ritual

How to brew it right

Hot and bold, or cold and sassy — it works both ways.

1

Heat

Water at 95°C — just off boil.

2

Measure

2 tsp per cup. Fruit needs room to open up.

3

Steep

5–7 minutes. No over-steep risk, it only gets better.

4

Chill (optional)

Pour over ice with a sprig of mint for the best iced tea you'll ever make.

The Story

Technicolour in a cup

Freeze-dried berry pieces lock in peak fruit flavour — no artificial anything. Pour a cup and watch it turn the water into stained glass.
