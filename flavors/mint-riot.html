The Ritual

How to brew it right

Gentle is the secret. Green tea punishes impatience.

1

Heat

Water to 80°C — just before boiling. Rolling boil will turn it bitter.

2

Measure

1 heaped tsp per cup. No more, no less.

3

Steep

2–3 minutes. Watch the leaves open up.

4

Sip

Straight, no milk. Try iced with a sprig of fresh mint.

The Story

Peak bracing energy

Mint Riot came out of a hot August afternoon when the team needed something loud but lifted. We walked into a Darjeeling field, grabbed a handful of wild mint, crushed it over a fresh batch of green leaves and knew.

Each pack is finished with a final dry-blend of peppermint oil-bearing leaves so the aroma hits you the moment you rip the seal.
