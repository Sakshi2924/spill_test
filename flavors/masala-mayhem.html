The Ritual

How to brew it right

Masala chai is about patience. Don't steep — simmer. Let the spices open up.

1

Boil

1 cup water, 2 tsp Masala Mayhem. Bring to a gentle rolling boil.

2

Simmer

Add 1 cup milk. Reduce heat and simmer for 3–4 minutes.

3

Sweeten

Jaggery, sugar or honey to taste. Stir through.

4

Pour

Strain into your favourite cup. Drink while it's still loud.

The Story

Chai, but make it loud

Masala Mayhem was born in a cramped kitchen in Assam at 5 a.m., when our founder was tired of chai that tasted like nothing. So she threw in more cardamom. More pepper. A whole lot more attitude.

Every batch is blended by hand in small lots. We toast the spices just before packing so each pouch opens with that unmistakable first-day-of-monsoon smell.
