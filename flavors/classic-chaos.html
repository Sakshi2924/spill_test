The Ritual

How to brew it right

Simple is hardest. Get these four right and you'll never need another cup.

1

Boil

Fresh cold water to a rolling boil. Don't reboil.

2

Measure

1 tsp per cup. Scale up by cup count — not by strength.

3

Steep

3–4 minutes. Longer goes bitter. Trust the timer.

4

Finish

Milk and sugar, lemon, or straight — all work. It's your cup.

The Story

The one that started it all

Classic Chaos is sourced from a single estate in Upper Assam that's been tea country since 1863. The leaves are plucked only during the second-flush summer window, when flavour peaks.

We don't blend it. We don't cut it. The pouch you get is the estate's own orthodox batch — the way tea was meant to taste before the industry flattened it out.
