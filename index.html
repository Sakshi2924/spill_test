The India problem

Summer in India is
getting harder to bear

India logged its hottest summer on record in 2024. Temperatures crossed 45°C in over a dozen states. And yet, most households face the same three walls.

Everair was built specifically for the Indian bedroom — the heat, the load-shedding, the rented apartments, and the electricity bills that spiral by May.

🌡️

Unbearable heat, rising every year

45°C+ summers are now common across UP, Bihar, Rajasthan, Maharashtra, and Telangana. Fans and coolers aren't enough anymore.

🔌

Power cuts hit hardest at night

Load-shedding in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities runs 4–8 hours a day in summer. Standard ACs go off when the grid does. Sleep becomes impossible.

🏠

Standard AC installation is a commitment

Wall drilling, HVAC technicians, landlord permissions, ₹10,000+ in installation fees. Renters simply can't afford the process.

💸

Electricity bills are already sky high

A 1-ton split AC draws 800–1,500W. Running one through a power cut on an inverter drains it in 1–2 hours. The math just doesn't work.

What's inside

Built for Indian summers.
Engineered for Indian realities.

Every spec was chosen because it solves a real problem faced by real Indian households.

🔋

Modular 1 kWh LiFePO4 battery — sleep through any power cut

The built-in lithium iron phosphate battery pack provides 5–8 hours of uninterrupted sleep cooling at steady-state 120–180W draw. LiFePO4 chemistry means safe, long-life cells that won't overheat — even in a hot Indian bedroom. Add extra battery modules as your needs grow.

⚡

3,000 BTU at just 200–350W

Standard 1-ton ACs consume 800–1,500W. Everair delivers targeted bedroom cooling at a fraction of the power — making battery backup actually practical and your electricity bill dramatically lower.

🔩

48V DC-native — no inverter losses

The compressor runs directly on 48V DC from the battery, eliminating the 15–25% energy lost in DC→AC→DC inverter conversion. More of your battery goes toward cooling, not heat.

🪟

Window-sitter form factor — 10 minutes, zero tools

Slide the unit onto your windowsill, close the window around it, and plug in. No drilling, no technician, no landlord permission, no ₹10,000 installation bill. Move it to another room or take it when you move house.

Setup in 3 steps

From box to cool air
in 10 minutes flat

No appointments. No waiting. No professional required. If you can open a window, you can set up Everair.

Unbox and position

Lift Everair out of the box and place it on your windowsill. The unit fits standard Indian window frames (24"–48" wide). No measuring required.

⏱ 3 minutes
02
🪟

Close window, seal gaps

Close your window panels around the unit. Use the included foam strips to seal gaps and block hot outside air. No tools, no drilling, no permanent changes.

⏱ 5 minutes

Plug into any standard 5A socket. Set your temperature. When the power goes out, the unit seamlessly switches to battery. You won't even wake up.
